# Tadomah Hotel Web Project

## Project Overview
Tadomah Hotel is a web-based project designed to showcase the amenities, rooms, services, and unique experiences offered by Tadomah Hotel, located in Tantra Hills, Accra. The site provides detailed information about the hotel, including booking options, gallery, restaurant, bar, special offers, and contact details.

## Features
- **Home Page:** Introduction to Tadomah Hotel and its unique appeal for business and leisure travelers.
- **About Us:** Learn about the hotel's history, vision, and what makes it special.
- **Rooms & Suites:** Explore a variety of rooms and suites with images and descriptions.
- **Amenities:** List of amenities such as pool, free Wi-Fi, free parking, laundry, breakfast, restaurant, conference space, and more.
- **Gallery:** Visual tour of the hotel’s facilities and events.
- **Restaurant & Bar:** Information about dining options, menus, and operating hours.
- **Special Offers:** Promotions and exclusive deals for guests.
- **Booking:** Online reservation interface.
- **Contact:** Email, phone, and address for inquiries and support.
- **Newsletter Subscription:** Stay updated with latest offers and news.
- **Social Media Links:** Connect via Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

## Technology Stack
- **HTML5** for structure
- **CSS3** for styling (custom stylesheets in `/css`)
- **JavaScript** for interactivity (scripts in `/javascript`)
- **Images** stored in `/images` directory
