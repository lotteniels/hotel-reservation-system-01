How to find us?

Located at coordinates 5.648309894332994 latitude and -0.2571373268530977 longitude, Tadomah Hotel in Accra offers a strategically positioned urban retreat. Situated near the Nsawam Main Road and close to the Taifa Overpass, the hotel provides easy access to leisure and business activities. Its proximity to these key landmarks ensures convenience for guests, allowing them to explore the vibrant cityscape and attend events with ease. The surrounding area, characterized by the dynamic intersection of the Nsawam Main Road and the Taifa Overpass, enhances the hotel's accessibility and enriches the overall stay.
